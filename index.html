Welcome!
Everyone knows that the most important part of your work- out is the WARM UP! This game will help you to do your warm up properly to avoid any injures later.
INSTRUCTIONS:
On the wall you will see coloured circles, which you need to touch while they are still visible. When you touch the circle you get one point from each, which will be counted in the right screen corner. The more points you have the better warm up you did.
You have to choose a difficulty level according to your needs. The higher level you choose the faster circles will come and disappear.
TO START THE GAME YOU NEED:
Choose your current activity level;
Choose your favourite Playlist;
Push START button;
